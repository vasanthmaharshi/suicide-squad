Reflection

Come into an alliance with your partner and arm yourselves with all the grooves and moves, and prove it to us that you got what it takes to be a doublet for each other dancing against the world.

Rules-:
  • Duration of each performance is 2.5-5 min.
  • There will be a single showcase round.
    • Participants are allowed to use props for a part of their performance. No props will be provided by event organizers.
  • Slots will be given prior to the event and will be random.
  • Dance performance should not convey any indecent gestures.
  • Participants are requested to submit their tracks in .mp3 format well in advance as informed by the organizing committee.
Judging Criteria -:
  • Choreography 30
  • Synchronization 25
  • Energy 20
  • Overall Impact 15
  • Creativity 10
